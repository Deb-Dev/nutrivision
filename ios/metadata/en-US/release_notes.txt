NutriVision v2.2.0 - Typography Enhancement Release (May 27, 2025)

NEW FEATURES:
- Centralized typography system with 10 Google Fonts (Roboto, Open Sans, Lato, Montserrat, Source Sans Pro, Nunito, Poppins, Inter, Raleway, Ubuntu)

- Material Design 3 compliant text styles throughout the app

IMPROVEMENTS:
- Standardized typography across all screens
- Enhanced visual consistency and readability
- Better accessibility through proper text hierarchy
- Improved user customization options
